Careers

Our career options

In a typical game development company, the company's sales are the first thing that determines the content of the project and the projects to participate in, but Synapse Studio is different. We give top priority to the "what we want to do" of the creators we belong to, and we try to find a job that meets the creator's wishes as much as possible.

We offer a new normal way of working that can only be done by C&R's own studio!

Interviews

What our creators say about the studio

"It is a workplace where you can be involved in a wide variety of projects, so I think it is attractive because there are many opportunities for those who are motivated to improve their skills. I think it is unique to our company that there are people who have changed jobs from other occupations, and people who have gained experience and participated in large-scale projects."

UI/UX Designer

12 years of experience

"Synapse Studio does business with development companies all over Kansai, so if you are unsure about finding a job at a game company in the region, I think it is a good idea to come to Synapse first and experience working at various sites. We also have a system that is not often seen, such as a career advancement course that encourages you to change jobs and become independent, so we hope that you will use the studio more and more for your own career advancement!"

HR Manager

15 years of experience

"We create animations according to instructions and storyboards, and we also come up with new animations and create them. Having said that, recently I have been working not only on animation work but also on modeling and effects, so I am involved in a wide range of work!"

Animator

7 years of experience

"To be honest, I was thinking that the work would be more top-down, corresponding to a set job. After joining the company, I had a lot of freedom and discretion, and I was surprised because there were many opportunities for my self-communication to be reflected. However (although you will gradually step up according to your experience), you will be required to take on a certain amount of responsibility and achievements."

UI/UX Designer

8 years of experience
