Contract Development

Our creators can develop the work ordered by the client at our studio. Since we use our in-house development environment to carry out our work in Japan, it is possible to keep the cost of development and equipment low. In addition, it is possible to stay at the client's site for about 2~3 months to understand the status of the project or provide training.

Read More

Secondment

Our creators can be stationed at the client's site to carry out the work. It's possible to switch to contract development per the client's request.
