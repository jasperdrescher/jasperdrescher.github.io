History

Our history

Synapse Studio was established on September 1, 2014 in Osaka, Japan. Synapse means the junction between nerves and the prefix "syn" means "together."

There were only three founding members, all of whom were graphic designers. From there, the number of creators gradually increased, and as of 2025, we are a game development studio with more than 50 creators.

We have a track record of developing small-scale games and game assets for our clients. Recently, we have started developing in-house games and collaborating on full scale game development together with other C&R studios. With a robust portfolio boasting over 50 successfully delivered projects, we have demonstrated our ability to iterate and execute, consistently meeting our clients' objectives and surpassing expectations.

Our studio is operated by Creek & River as part of C&R's Creative Studios group.

Values

What we value most

Improving the value of creators

"Improving the value of creators" is C&R's overall philosophy, and we will do everything necessary to realize it. From the cradle to the grave, creators continue to be creative, which is the core philosophy of our studio.

Function as a joint

In the studio, people from all walks of life come together to do development. Like the meaning of synapse, each brand name expresses the desire to "function as a joint".

Contributing to the creation of value for clients

To put it simply, we are a company that is working hard to protect an environment where creators can eat properly while doing creative work

Services

What we offer

Versatility

We have contributed to the creation of a wide range of Japanese games, encompassing mobile, casual, VR, and AAA titles. Our team, comprising more than 1000 talented creators, is proficient in various disciplines, such as 2D and 3D design. We possess the versatility to work on games that feature diverse styles, from Japanese-inspired to Western-inspired aesthetics.

Efficiency

We offer services that are cost-effective, considering the labor expenses in Japan―this could result in more affordable options compared to hiring local assistance for our clients overseas. Our team includes proficient English speakers and translators to ensure seamless international communication.

Flexibility

Whether you need just one person or a team of ten, we're here to collaborate with you. We have a wide range of professionals, from junior to senior developers, including team leaders and project managers. Our services span from the main game development to testing and ensuring the game's quality by offering advice to enhance your game's key features.

Dynamic Staffing

Synapse Studio's team comprises over 50 professionals with diverse backgrounds, specialized expertise, and perspectives, ensuring a rich blend of skills to tackle any challenge with creativity and innovation.
