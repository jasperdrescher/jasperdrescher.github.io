About me

Passionate and goal-oriented Software Engineer with more than 5 years of professional experience in game development. I specialize in C++ and C# for Windows, Xbox Series X, PlayStation 5, Android and iOS. I graduated from Breda Univesity of Applied Sciences in 2020, where I developed multiple games during my studies. Recently, I worked at Ubisoft in Osaka and Malmö, where I developed AAA games for multiple platforms. At Ubisoft Osaka I was able to focus more in engine programming, while at Massive Entertainment in Malmö my focus was on gameplay programming. Before that, I worked at CortexArts to develop serious games, such as Otter Space.
